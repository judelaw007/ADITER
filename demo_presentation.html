How It Works

üîÑ The Complete Workflow

Step 1: Content Extraction

The system parses your DOCX study notes and intelligently converts them into presentation slides using heading structures.

Step 2: Voice Generation

OpenAI's TTS-1-HD model creates high-quality MP3 narration for each slide with your choice of 6 professional voices.

Step 3: Synchronization

Each slide is paired with its audio file. When you navigate to a slide, the audio automatically starts.

üéØ Example: A 20-slide ADIT module converts to an interactive presentation in under 5 minutes, with full voice narration costing approximately $0.30.

Encryption & Security

üîí Password-Protected Delivery

Your presentations can be encrypted with AES-256 encryption for secure distribution:

Security Features:

  • PBKDF2 Key Derivation - 100,000 iterations
